AstroSweepers

A reusable debris-clearing satellite concept that captures, processes, and recycles orbital debris in low Earth orbit.

Overview

AstroSweepers is designed around a simple idea: orbital debris should not only be removed, it should be converted into useful mission material. The satellite uses high-resolution imaging and AI-assisted navigation to detect debris, robotic arms to capture it, and an onboard processing chamber to separate recyclable material.

Core Features

AI-assisted debris detection and navigation.
Robotic capture arms for controlled collection.
Solar-powered heat chamber for metallic debris processing.
Reusable mission model where recycled metals can support propulsion.

Focus

Concept design, system architecture, sustainability, and autonomous operation in low Earth orbit.
