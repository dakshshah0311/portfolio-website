02 — Sustainable Systems

Net Zero
Campus

An AI-enabled dashboard concept that connects IoT sensors, solar data, waste management, and carbon reporting into one operating view.

Overview

Net Zero Campus brings campus sustainability data into a single dashboard. It is planned to ingest sensor readings, solar production, utility usage, and waste logs, then use predictive analytics to forecast demand and suggest or trigger operational improvements.

Core Features

Unified dashboard for energy, waste, solar, and carbon data.
Predictive analytics for energy demand and waste trends.
Automated eco-alerts and recycling schedule suggestions.
Campus-level carbon footprint tracking.

Stack

React.jsTensorFlowIoTFirebaseAnalytics

Focus

Dashboard UX, real-time data, sustainability automation, and predictive decision support.
